The Town of Middleton provides children with several recreational activities, such as Little League Baseball and Softball, and Middleton Youth Soccer.

The Middleton Recreation Summer Park Program offers the children of Middleton a variety of activities over the summer, such as arts and crafts and sports. The kids get to hang out with their friends and have fun.
